My Story

Hi! Welcome to my portfolio website! My Name's Sevda. I recently graduated from Coder Academy's bootcamp with a Diploma of Information Technology and I looking forward to starting my career as a web developer.

I come from a science and research background and have worked both in academia and industry however I were never satisfied with what I was doing until I decided to build a website to showcase my paintings!

At first I was only supposed to use one of the content management websites, however while I was watching tutorials on Youtube I got hooked on coding!. The challanges I was facing gave me a great satisfaction. I started to learn more and more and one day I decided to quit my full-time job and pursue the coding path.

I am so happy I took the risk and grateful to all the people who encouraged and supported me. So here I am, a code newbie, excited to to work on challanging applications...

Interests

I have a passion for painting and try to paint in my sparetime. I mainly use acrylic paints on canvas and willing to start using oil colors in near future.

I also enjoy reading classic literature, autobiographies, science-fiction books and watching comedy TV showes.

I love making puzzles and the little victories after each challange are very empowering. I have the same feeling when I code!

... and when I am not coding you can find me baking! I bake pretty good!
